Henry Kenney (Upham map #28)

Most probably Henry, Sr., he was born ca. 1624. Kenney settled in Salem Village about 1648 and served as a soldier in 1654 at the capture of St. John. He was a yeoman and died after 1705. A son, Henry, Jr., was born in 1669 and was living on his father¹s homestead in 1692, a year after marrying Priscilla Lewis. (Richard Trask, The Devil Hath Been Raised, 1997, p. 127.)
