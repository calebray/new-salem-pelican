Elizabeth Parris, Jr. (Upham map #"149")

In 1692 Betty was the middle child of Samuel and Elizabeth Parris between Thomas (b. 1681) and Susannah (b. 1687). She was born in Boston on November 28, 1682. Grievously "afflicted" early on, Betty was sent by late march 1692 to live in Salem at the Stephen Sewall house. In 1710 in Sudbury she married Benjamin Barron. (Richard Trask, The Devil Hath Been Raised, 1997, p. 128.)
