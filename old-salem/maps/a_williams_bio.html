Abigail Williams (Upham map # "149")

A kinswoman described as a niece of Rev. Parris, Williams was a servant in the household and aged about 12 in 1692. Though some have speculated about a family connection with Rev. Roger Williams, her family background and post-1692 history are unknown. (Richard Trask, The Devil Hath Been Raised, 1997, p. 131.)
