Daniel Rea (Upham map # 48)

An Indian fighter in King Philip¹s War, Daniel Rea (b. March 30. 1654; d. March 5, 1714-15) was named after his grandfather, Daniel Rea, who was one of the original settlers in Salem Village in 1686. Daniel and his wife Hepzibah signed the petition written by their neighbor, Isreal Porter, in defense of Rebecca Nurse.
