4. Salem Village & Salem Town Record Books, 1630s to 1700s

  • Salem Village Record Book 1686-1713 fully transcribed in several issues of the Hist. Coll. of Danvers Historical Society, 1925ff., permission to scan has been granted, OCR scanning process completed. Word text in production 5/10.
  • Salem Village Church Record Book 19 Nov.1698 - 15 Jan 1692, NEHGS&R Vol. 11, 1857; pp. 131- 135; pp. 316 - 321, 25 Jan 1692 - 19 Jan 1700. Vol. 12, pp. 245 - 148, 19 Jan 1700 - 248. Vol. 13, pp. 55-56, 5 Sept 1746 - 13 May 1753. Page 56 list of deaths in Salem Village, 1688 - 1700. Now fully OCR and put into Word text form, most corrected - 5/10.
  • Town Records of Salem, 1634-1659, E. I. H. C., Vol. 9, 1869, pp. 6 - 232.

Later Town Records are in Vol. 83 (1947) contains Records of 1692 period.

  • Records of the First Church in Salem, Essex Institute, 1974. Relevant excerpts
  • Rev. Joseph Green, Diary: E. I. H. C., vol. 8 (1866), 91-96, 165-175; Vol. 10 (1869), 73-104; Vol. 26 (1900), 323-330.
  • Rev. Joseph Green, Common Place Book, Publications of the Colonial Soc. off Mass. 1943 Vol 34, 191-253.

6. Significant Secondary Sources

compendium of genealogical information and pictures of houses & places.

  • Nathaniel Hawthorne, excerpts from books & essays, describing historical recollections of various participants -- accusers, accused.
