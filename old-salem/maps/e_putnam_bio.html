Edward Putnam (Upham map #23)

The younger brother of Thomas Putnam, Jr., Edward was born in 1654 to the village¹s richest couple, Lieutenant Thomas and Ann (Holyoke) Putnam. A yeoman, Putnam served as one of two deacons at the Salem Village Church. He died in 1747. (Richard Trask, The Devil Hath Been Raised, 1997, p. 130).
