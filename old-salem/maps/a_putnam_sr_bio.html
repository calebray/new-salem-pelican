Ann Putnam, Sr. (Upham map #24)

The youngest daughter of George and Elizabeth Carr of Salisbury, Massachusetts, Ann Carr was born in 1661 and married thomas Putnam in 1678. Her sister, maryCarr, was the wife of the first Salem Village minister, James Bayley. In 1692 Ann and Thomas had six living children, a seventh having died in 1689. Pregnant during much of 1692, Ann gave birth to Abigail prior to October 30 of that year. Ann, Sr. died in 1699 just two weeks after her husband¹s death. (Richard Trask, The Devil Hath Been Raised, 1997, p. 129.)
