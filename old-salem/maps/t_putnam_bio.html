Thomas Putnam (Upham map #24)

The eldest son of Lieutenant Thomas Putnam, Thomas, Jr., was born in 1652. He had fought in the King Philip¹s War. A yeoman and prominent village inhabitant, Putnam has served for many years as parish clerk. With his wife, he was one of the founding signers of the 1689 Salem Village Church Covenant. He died in 1699. (Richard Trask, The Devil Hath Been Raised, 1997, p. 130.)
